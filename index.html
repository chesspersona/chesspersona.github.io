Introduction

ChessPersona is an innovative platform designed to unravel the complex fabric of chess playing styles through the lens of advanced data analysis. We aim to bridge the gap between raw game data and actionable insights, empowering players, coaches, and chess enthusiasts to understand and refine their strategic approach to the game.

Feature Extraction

Center Control Score

Measures control over key central squares.

Piece Activity Score

Assesses the mobility and activity of pieces throughout the game.

King Safety Score

Evaluates the safety of the king based on surrounding pieces and pawn structure.

Attacking Moves Score

Quantifies the aggressiveness of play.

Captures Score

Tracks the number of captures made by a player, indicating tactical sharpness.

Pawn Structure Stability Score

Analyzes the stability and weakness in the pawn structure.

Machine Learning

The ChessPersona application uses machine learning to analyze chess playing styles. It provides insights by training on a dataset of games played by grandmasters and categorizing play styles based on extracted features. This process helps in understanding the tactical and strategic nuances that differentiate players.

K-means Clustering

A popular clustering method that groups data based on feature similarity.

DBSCAN

Useful for identifying clusters of arbitrary shape and for handling noise and outliers.
